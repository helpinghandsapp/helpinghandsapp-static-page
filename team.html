Meet The Team

Here at Helping Hands we stand-out by tailoring our organization-youth matching services to include the desired skills the youth volunteers would like to develop. Our team of experienced professional works together to form healthy relationships between nonprofit organizations and volunteers or students who are looking for a community service placement.
