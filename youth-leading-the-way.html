YOUTH LEADING THE WAY

PROGRAM

Do you want more volunteering experiences?

Helping Hands is running a new enhanced volunteer program running into March 2024. In this program, you can be matched to a long-term volunteer opportunity where you can gain valuable experience, skills, and long-term connections that can help you with your career. This program comes with extra financial support that is not covered in any of Helping Hands' other programs. For your participation in this program, you can receive some recognition and compensation.

Youth Leading the Way Program (now till March 2024) entails...

  1. Free volunteer matching services for 60 youth participants to volunteer service placement(s) to fulfill 120 hours of community involvement.
  2. Training sessions and mentorship provided by Helping Hands (which also counts towards the 120 hours) for youth participants
  3. Financial aid provided to youth by Helping Hands help them participate in this program.
  4. Youth participants can gain valuable experience, recognition, and long-term connections from this program
For any questions, please email outreach@helpinghandsapp.com
