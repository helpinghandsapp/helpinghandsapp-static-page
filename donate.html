Make a Difference

At Helping Hands, we work with the leaders of tomorrow—Black, low-income, and newcomer youth who are passionate about serving their communities. We harness the power of volunteering to increase youth civic engagement and career development. But we can’t do this alone! Donations from community members like you are crucial. Without your donations, our youth-led work would not be possible.

Many young people ask us, “I have so much going on—how do I complete my community service hours in time for graduation?” or “How do I find a mentor to guide me in my career?” With your support, we can help these youth achieve their goals. Every donation counts; each young person we support will support others in their community. Join us in our mission to uplift the leaders of tomorrow!
