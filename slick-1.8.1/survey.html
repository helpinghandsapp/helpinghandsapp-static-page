Creating Expriential Learning Opportunities for Black Youth

Are you Passionate About Creating Meaningful Opportunities for Black Youth?

We invite you to participate in our important research study.

We are actively seeking Canadian adults who identify as either Black families (caregivers or parents) or community stakeholders in education, business, and government across Toronto.

By taking part in this survey, you will have a unique chance to contribute your insights, experiences, and perspectives to the design of high-quality experiential learning programs for Black youth. Your valuable input will help us co-create opportunities that foster knowledge, essential skills, work habits, technical literacy, and community networks.

Your voice matters. Take the first step by participating in our survey today and help us build a better tomorrow for Black youth. Together, we can create a brighter future filled with empowerment, growth, and success.
