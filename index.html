Our Mission

Helping Hands Platform (Helping Hands) is a grassroots, youth-led not-for-profit organization that works to increase youth community engagement!

Helping Hands aims to help young people build their skills and careers through volunteer, entrepreneurship, and early career exploration. Ontario youth between the ages of 13–29 have access to our services free of charge.
