Help Keep The Hands Helping! Here at Helping Hands, we work constantly to help youth to find relevant volunteer opportunities and needed community involvement, but we might not be able to continue without you! Your charitable gift to Helping Hands will support us in our attempts to connect students and volunteers with organizations where they can develop the skills they need. There are numerous students who are asking themselves "Where to volunteer" or "How to find volunteer placement". With your support, Helping Hands will be able to assist more of them. Matching a volunteer job that fits you perfectly is amazing and it could turn someone's life all around for the better. So let's help people to change their lives together!
